Appendix B · risks & mitigation

13

Key risks are known — and already bounded by practical mitigation

Risk 01

Copilot Studio RAG remains a black box

Start with documentation reorganization now; keep a self-hosted RAG fallback if the black-box limit becomes material.

Risk 02

No CMDB / Service Catalog baseline today

Make Service Catalog MVP part of phase 1 instead of treating it as a dependency someone else must solve first.

Risk 03

LLM variability may produce unsafe repair suggestions

Use evidence chains, confidence thresholds, and mandatory human-in-the-loop approval for production actions.

Risk 04

Cloud billing tags are not aligned enough for deeper UC3 work

Run tag validation and data-quality cleanup before moving into UC3 phase 2 automation.
