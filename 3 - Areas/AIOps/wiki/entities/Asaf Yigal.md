# Asaf Yigal

**Domain:** Observability, AIOps, DevOps

## Background

Asaf Yigal is the co-founder and Chief Product Officer of [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/entities/Logz.io]], an AI-powered observability platform. He is a practitioner voice in the observability community, writing on topics including the failure modes of [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/concepts/AIOps]] and the promise of GenAI-enhanced observability workflows.

---

## Contributions to This Knowledge Base

- **[[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/sources/[2024-10-28] AI-powered observability picking up where AIOps failed]]** — argued that AIOps failed due to organisational resistance (not tech failure), and drew a clear conceptual line between AI-first AIOps and observability-first AI-enhanced tooling.

---

## Key Positions

- AIOps failure was an *adoption* problem, not a *technology* problem
- GenAI in observability should be framed as an enhancement layer, not a wholesale replacement of observability discipline
- Natural language interfaces are the key to democratising observability beyond technical specialists
