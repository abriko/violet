# Alolita Sharma

**Role:** Open-source engineer, observability & AI advocate  
**Organisation:** [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/entities/CNCF]] (Cloud Native Computing Foundation)  
**Known for:** KubeCon talk on [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/concepts/Intelligent Observability]] (2024-03-22)

---

## Overview

Alolita Sharma is an open-source engineer and CNCF contributor who has spoken at KubeCon on the convergence of cloud-native observability with AI. She coined or popularised the term **[[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/concepts/Intelligent Observability]]** in the context of cloud-native AI-enabled applications.

---

## Key Contribution to This Wiki

Her 2024-03-22 KubeCon talk establishes a prerequisite chain:

```
Cloud-Native Observability → Intelligent Observability → AIOps
```

She argues that supporting AI models as first-class observable workloads — understanding system behaviour qualitatively, not just quantitatively — is the foundational step before true [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/concepts/AIOps]] can be achieved.
