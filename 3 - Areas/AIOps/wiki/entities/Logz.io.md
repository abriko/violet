# Logz.io

**Type:** Observability platform (commercial SaaS)  
**Founded by:** [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/entities/Asaf Yigal]] (co-founder / CPO)  
**Domain:** AI-powered observability — logs, metrics, traces

---

## Overview

Logz.io is a cloud-native AI-powered observability platform built on open-source foundations (ELK stack, OpenSearch). It integrates GenAI capabilities to surface insights from telemetry data through natural language interfaces, anomaly detection, and automated troubleshooting suggestions.

Logz.io is notable for positioning itself explicitly as "AI-powered observability" (observability-first + AI as enhancement) rather than traditional [[3 - Areas/AIOps/wiki/concepts/AIOps]] (AI-first with exhaustive data ingestion).
